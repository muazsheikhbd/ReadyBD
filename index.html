Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about free personal domains.

Is it really free? What's the catch?+

Yes, it is 100% free for personal use. The service is supported by community donations and sponsorships. No hidden fees and no credit card required.

Can I use it for my business website?+

No. These domains are strictly for personal, non-commercial use only. Business or commercial use violates the terms and can result in suspension.

Do I own the domain name?+

You get full usage rights as long as you comply with the terms. Technically these are subdomains, but you can use them indefinitely for free.

What DNS records are supported?+

A, AAAA, CNAME, TXT, MX, NS, and SRV records are supported, so you can point your domain to the hosting service you prefer.

Can I use the domain for email?+

Yes. You can set up MX records with any email provider or use the email forwarding option when available.
